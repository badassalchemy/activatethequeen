🔮 Journal Prompts
Prompt 01 · Permission
Where in my life am I still asking for permission to exist as myself?
The Queen doesn't shrink to be understood. Name the rooms, relationships, or choices where you've been softening your truth — and what it would look like to stand fully in who you are.

Prompt 02 · The Becoming
Who is the woman I am becoming — the one I respect?
Describe her in detail. How does she move? How does she speak? What does she allow herself to want, to receive, to expect? This is the identity you're stepping into.
✓ Saved
Prompt 03 · Receiving
What am I ready to receive — without guilt, struggle, or proof?
Money. Love. Ease. Opportunity. Name it specifically. Then name what you're releasing to make room for it — the shame, the chase, the fear of wanting more.
